Things to Check - 

Here’s a clean, organized checklist you can use when evaluating property management companies:

---

## **Property Management Company Evaluation Checklist**

### **1. Fees & Costs**

* Management fees
* Fees during vacancy
* Leasing fees (for finding a tenant)
* Lease renewal fees
* Maintenance markups (if any)
* Early termination fees

---

### **2. Contract Terms**

* Contract length
* Termination clause (notice period, penalties)
* Authorization needed before leasing the property
* Approval threshold for repairs (e.g., need owner approval for costs above $___)

---

### **3. Tenant Placement & Screening**

* Marketing strategy (MLS, Zillow, social media, signage, etc.)
* Typical time to lease a property
* Tenant screening process (credit, background, income, rental history)
* Lease creation and signing process

---

### **4. Maintenance & Repairs**

* How maintenance requests are handled
* In-house vs. third-party vendors
* Emergency repair procedures
* Inspection schedule (move-in, move-out, periodic)
* Whether inspection reports include photos/videos

---

### **5. Communication & Tools**

* Owner portal availability
* Monthly statements and financial reporting
* Response time to owner and tenant inquiries
* Point of contact (single manager vs. team)

---

### **6. Rent Collection & Legal Handling**

* Rent collection process
* Late payment and delinquency handling
* Eviction process and associated fees
* Required insurance (landlord policy, renter’s insurance)

---

### **7. Owner Payments**

* When you receive rent payments each month
* Method of deposit (ACH, check)
